This page contains a small list of Japanese flowers
  • Sakura (Cherry Blossom) – pink or white flowers which bloom in clusters on cherry trees, typically blooming in early spring.
  • Fuji (Wisteria) – vines with cascading clusters of purple, white, pink, or blue flowers which bloom in late spring.
  • Kiku (Chrysanthemum) – perennial flowering plant which blooms in shades of yellow, white, red, and purple during autumn.
  • Higanbana (Red Spider Lily) – flowers with bright red petals that extend outward in a spidery shape, typically blooming in early autumn.
  • Ajisai (Hydrangea) – shrub that produces globe-like clusters of flowers in shades of blue, purple, pink, or white.
